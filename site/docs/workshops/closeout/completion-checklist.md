# Closeout Completion Checklist

Work through this checklist after you have completed all four movements of the
[Closeout part](./index.md). Every item is checkable by inspecting a specific artifact — not by how
the session felt.

When every item is checked, you have completed the three-part program.

---

## Carry-forward artifact

- [ ] You have a carry-forward artifact as a **separate document** — the learnings-capture
      worksheet is not the artifact; the artifact is a standalone document you can open cold at
      the start of a future project
- [ ] The artifact contains at least **one reusable pattern** with all three elements filled: what
      it is, where it helped in this project, and when it would and would not transfer to a
      different project
- [ ] The artifact contains at least **one dead end** with the early signal named — not just what
      went wrong, but what to watch for earlier next time
- [ ] The artifact contains **workflow reflections**: where spec-first planning and autonomous
      delegation paid off, and where they were overhead for this project's actual size or shape
- [ ] The artifact reads **coherently without this project's context** — a reader starting a
      similar project without having been in this one would find it useful

---

## Brief presentation

- [ ] You have filled in all three elements of the
      [presentation worksheet](./presentation-worksheet.md): what you built, your single most
      important learning, and what you would do differently
- [ ] Element 1 (what you built) is **one or two sentences** a non-expert could follow
- [ ] Element 2 (your most important learning) is **specific and transferable** — it names a
      concrete insight, not a general impression, and it draws from your carry-forward artifact
- [ ] Element 3 (what you would do differently) is **one concrete change**, not a vague intention
- [ ] The three elements together fit within a **5-minute time box** when spoken aloud (or are
      equivalently concise in written form for a self-paced share)

---

## All items checked?

If every item above is checked, you have completed the Closeout part and the program.

**What you now have:**

- A plan you produced in the Planning part
- A project you executed in the Building part, proven with a single verification command
- A carry-forward artifact capturing what the next project needs to know
- A brief presentation of your results

The carry-forward artifact is what distinguishes finishing the program from finishing the project.
When you start your next project, open it first.
